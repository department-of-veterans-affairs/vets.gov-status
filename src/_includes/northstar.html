Our North Star
VA.gov was created to provide a single unified digital experience for all Veterans. This performance dashboard tracks our progress in that mission by sharing metrics related to our three North Star goals: 1) increasing the use of self-service tools, measured by the number of logged-in users on VA.gov, 2) decreasing the time Veterans spend waiting for an outcome, measured by successful transactions on VA.gov and 3) consistently delivering a satisfying, reliable, and secure experience, measured by customer satisfaction.
